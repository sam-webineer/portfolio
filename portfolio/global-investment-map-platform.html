Global investment map platform

The brief

Allianz Global Investors sought a way to showcase their extensive portfolio of global investments, through an interactive and fun way.

Finding the right solution

Working in a close team, we devised the solution of developing an interactive map interface, dynamically showing all the investment stories, locations and information across the globe, with a layer of customisation through filtering.

As the dedicated senior web developer on the project, my challenge was to effectively deliver a solution that ensured effective management of the portfolio projects being showcased whilst bringing a comprehensive level of customization and interactivity for the client and the platforms users.

Putting it on the map

Applying my technical skillset, I successfully produced a fully responsive interactive map website that uses JavaScript languages to digest a JSON driven data source and passed through the Google Maps API to a custom front-end endpoint. This solution ensured a lean approach to driving the data whilst giving users the ability to explore, interact and filter the global investment in a fun and effective way.
